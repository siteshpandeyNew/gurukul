Welcome to Gurukul Mission School

Established in 2008, Gurukul Mission School stands as a beacon of holistic education in Deoria, Uttar Pradesh. We blend the ancient Gurukul tradition of moral and spiritual learning with modern academic excellence under the CBSE framework.

Our institution nurtures students from Nursery to Class XII, providing them with a strong foundation in academics, values, and life skills. We believe every child has the potential to be a leader, and it is our mission to unlock that potential.

  • Experienced & Dedicated Faculty
