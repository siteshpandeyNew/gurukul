Admission Process

Follow these simple steps to enroll your child at Gurukul Mission School:

Step 1: Enquiry

Visit the school or call us to learn about available classes, curriculum, and facilities.

Step 2: Application Form

Fill the online application form or collect one from the school office.

Step 3: Document Submission

Submit all required documents along with the application form.

Step 4: Entrance Assessment

Students appear for an age-appropriate entrance assessment.

Step 5: Interaction

Parent-student interaction with the Principal and counsellor.

Step 6: Admission Confirmation

Pay the admission fee and complete the enrollment process.
