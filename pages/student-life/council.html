Student Council

Our Student Council is a platform for developing leadership, responsibility, and democratic values. Students are elected by their peers to represent and serve the student body.

Council Structure

  • Head Boy and Head Girl
  • House Captains and Vice Captains
  • Class Representatives (Monitors)
  • Sports Captain
  • Cultural Secretary
  • Discipline Prefects

Responsibilities

  • Organizing school events and celebrations
  • Maintaining discipline and school values
  • Representing student voice to the administration
  • Leading by example in academics and conduct
