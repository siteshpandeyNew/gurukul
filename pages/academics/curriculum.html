CBSE Curriculum Framework

Gurukul Mission School follows the comprehensive curriculum prescribed by CBSE with additional enrichment through value education, Vedic studies, and skill-based learning.

Subjects Offered

Primary (I-V)

English, Hindi, Mathematics, EVS, Computer Science, Art, Music, Physical Education, Value Education

Middle (VI-VIII)

English, Hindi, Sanskrit, Mathematics, Science, Social Science, Computer Science, Art, Physical Education

Secondary (IX-X)

English, Hindi, Mathematics, Science, Social Science + Optional: Sanskrit/Computer Applications

Senior Secondary (XI-XII)

Science: Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Maths, English
Commerce: Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics, English
Arts: History, Political Science, Geography, English
