Campus Security

Student safety is our utmost priority. We have implemented comprehensive security measures across the entire campus.

Security Measures

  • 24/7 CCTV surveillance covering all areas
  • Trained security personnel at all entry/exit points
  • Visitor management system with ID verification
  • Student ID cards mandatory for campus entry
  • Secure boundary walls around the campus
  • Fire safety equipment and regular drills
  • Emergency evacuation plans in place
  • Anti-bullying policy strictly enforced
  • Transport tracking for parent peace of mind
