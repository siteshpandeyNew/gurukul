Shri Ramesh Chandra Pandey

Chairman, Gurukul Mission School
Education is not the filling of a pail, but the lighting of a fire. At Gurukul Mission School, we ignite the flame of knowledge, values, and spiritual wisdom in every child.

Dear Parents and Students,

It gives me immense pride to lead an institution that stands firmly on the pillars of discipline, spirituality, and academic excellence. When we founded Gurukul Mission School in 2008, our vision was clear to create a school that would not just produce toppers, but responsible citizens who carry the light of Indian values wherever they go.

Over the years, I have watched our school grow from 150 students to over 2000, each one a testament to the dedication of our teachers and the trust of our parents. Our students consistently achieve outstanding results in board examinations and bring laurels through Olympiads, sports championships, and cultural competitions at the state and national levels.

I warmly invite you to be a part of the Gurukul Mission family. Together, let us shape the future leaders of our great nation.

With warm regards,
Shri Ramesh Chandra Pandey
