Our Story

Gurukul Mission School was established in 2008 with a clear vision — to create an educational institution that blends the timeless wisdom of the ancient Gurukul tradition with the best practices of modern pedagogy. Situated in Deoria, Uttar Pradesh, our school has grown from a humble beginning to a premier CBSE institution serving over 2000 students.

Founded by visionary educationists who believed in the power of value-based education, the school was conceived as a response to the growing need for institutions that nurture not just the mind, but also the heart and soul of every child.

Our Journey

2008 — Foundation Year

Gurukul Mission School opened its doors with 150 students and a dream to transform education in the region.

2012 — CBSE Affiliation

Received CBSE affiliation, marking a significant milestone in our academic journey.

2015 — Campus Expansion

Expanded campus with new science laboratories, computer labs, and sports facilities.

2018 — First Board Batch

Our first batch of Class XII students achieved 95%+ pass rate, setting the benchmark.

2023 — 2000+ Students

Crossed the milestone of 2000 enrolled students with consistently high academic results.

2026 — Continuing Excellence

Moving forward with smart classrooms, digital learning initiatives, and expanded extracurricular programs.
