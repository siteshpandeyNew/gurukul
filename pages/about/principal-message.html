Dr. Anita Sharma

Principal, Gurukul Mission School
A child is like a butterfly in the wind. Some can fly higher than others, but each one flies the best it can. Education should help every child find its wings.

Dear Students and Parents,

Welcome to Gurukul Mission School, where we believe in nurturing the complete individual. As Principal, my focus is on creating an environment where academic rigor meets creative exploration, and where every student feels valued and supported.

Our CBSE curriculum is enhanced with innovative teaching methodologies, project-based learning, and experiential activities. We integrate yoga, meditation, and value education into our daily schedule because we believe that a calm and focused mind is the foundation for all learning.

Our teachers are not just educators they are mentors, guides, and role models. They go above and beyond to ensure every student reaches their full potential. Our consistently excellent board results and numerous achievements in competitions are a reflection of this commitment.

I invite you to visit our campus, meet our faculty, and experience the Gurukul difference firsthand. Together, we can build a bright future for your child.

Warm regards,
Dr. Anita Sharma
